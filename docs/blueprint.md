## Core Features:

- Product Showcase: Display a range of office equipment, including printers and computers, available for sale or rent, with detailed specifications.
- Service Offerings: Detail the services provided, such as equipment rental, sales, and maintenance, each with a dedicated description and a call to action.
- Contact Form: A multi-field form that captures customer inquiries, including name, email, service of interest, and message, to facilitate direct communication.
- Interactive FAQ: Implement a chatbot tool to answer frequently asked questions about products, services, and support.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Red (#E62129) to reflect the 'H' from HyS and convey energy.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F9FAFB) in light mode, dark gray (#111827) in dark mode, for a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Orange (#F4AB1A), analogous to red, for highlighting interactive elements and CTAs.
- Headline font: 'Montserrat' (sans-serif) for a bold, modern display.
- Body font: 'Open Sans' (sans-serif) for clear, readable text.
- Use Material Icons for a consistent, recognizable visual language.
- Maintain a clean, grid-based layout to ensure readability and ease of navigation across devices.
